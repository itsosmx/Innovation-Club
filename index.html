Why Innovation Club?

Innovation clubs in universities help to engage students in innovative and creative activities apart from providing opportunities for practical activities. A culture of creativity and innovation for university students to be ready for the labor market and universities interested in the region. The main and immediate objective of the innovation club at the university is to create awareness, educate, nurture and instill a culture of innovation among university students and enable them to generate new ideas and become more creative.Through strategic partnership with science centers and universities, innovation clubs can reach our future generation,foster strong bonds with their students and embark on a critical mission of enhancing critical thinking and problem-solving abilities and adopting innovative methodology as part of their lifestyle.

Definition of Innovation

Innovation means to improve or to replace something, for example, a process, a product, or a service. ... Innovation is a process by which a domain, a product, or a service is renewed and brought up to date by applying new processes, introducing new techniques, or establishing successful ideas to create new value.
