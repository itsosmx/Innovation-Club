About Us

We, the second year of the Faculty of Computer Science, Nahda University, have started the Innovation Club
with the help of the Dean of the Faculty of Computer Science, Nahda University, Dr: Mohamed Ali Saleh Lead by eng:Hoda Joseph.
the Innovation Club aims to assist programmers and students participating in Nahda University, develop their skills and help them to create projects and enter the labor market by providing all the courses and trainings that the programmer needs to enter the labor market. Innovation clubs in universities help to engage students in innovative and creative activities apart from providing opportunities for practical activities. A culture of creativity and innovation for university students to be ready for the labor market and universities interested in the region. The main and immediate objective of the innovation club at the university is to create awareness, educate, nurture and instill a culture of innovation among university students and enable them to generate new ideas and become more creative.Through strategic partnership with science centers and universities, innovation clubs can reach our future generation,foster strong bonds with their students and embark on a critical mission of enhancing critical thinking and problem-solving abilities and adopting innovative methodology as part of their lifestyle.
